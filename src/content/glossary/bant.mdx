  BANT is lighter than MEDDPICC. It's designed for fast triage: does the prospect have budget, the authority to buy, an actual need, and a timeline? In tech sales interviews, BANT shows up most often in SDR/BDR rounds and in early-cycle qualification questions for AE candidates.

# BANT

BANT is a sales qualification framework: Budget, Authority, Need, Timing. Originally developed at IBM, it's still common in early-cycle qualification and SDR/BDR-to-AE handoffs.
