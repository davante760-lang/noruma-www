# The Challenger Sale

The Challenger Sale is a 2011 sales methodology by Brent Adamson and Matthew Dixon, based on CEB research. It argues that the most effective sellers don't build relationships first, they teach customers something new, tailor that insight to the customer's context, and take control of the conversation.
