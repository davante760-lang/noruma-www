  MEDDPICC and SPICED are both modern enterprise sales-qualification frameworks. MEDDPICC came out of legacy enterprise SaaS in the 1990s-2000s; SPICED was developed at Winning by Design as a discovery-first approach for SaaS in the 2010s.
